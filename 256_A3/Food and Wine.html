Seafood Odyssea Cruise

Relax and enjoy the stunning Marlborough Sounds with the exclusive afternoon Seafood Odyssea Cruise, departing Picton. As you cruise through Queen Charlotte Sound/Tōtaranui, sample local seafood such as Regal salmon, Cloudy Bay Clams and Greenshell mussels, with Marlborough sauvignon blanc for the definitive wine/food match.

Whakarewarewa The Living Maori Village

In traditional hāngī cooking, food such as fish and kumara (sweet potato), were cooked in a pit dug in the ground. Today, pork, lamb, potato, pumpkin and cabbage are also included. A modern hāngī is likely to use cloth sacks to wrap the food in, also aluminium foil and wire baskets.
